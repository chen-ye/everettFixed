City of Everett Logo Design Contest

 
1. Please select the five (5) designs that you feel best represent Everett. For a reminder of our design guidelines, visit www.everettwa.org/default.aspx?ID=2219. We received more than 800 submissions to the contest, so allow some time to review all of the designs. Online voting ends at 11:59 p.m. Tuesday, Oct. 28.

*** Note that the corresponding check box is ABOVE each logo. Click on the logo itself to ensure that you're choosing the right box. ***

Some submissions are artistic concepts and not fully realized designs. We will work with our graphic designers to finalize the winning design and use it to create the City's family of logos. Some artists submitted alternate versions of their designs and we have included those for reference where possible.
